Account Deletion

Request Deletion of Your Account and Data

If you wish to delete your ScanPest AI account and all associated data, please follow the process outlined below. We are committed to respecting your privacy and will permanently remove all your information from our systems.

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to:

  • All your pest detection history and results
  • All uploaded images and photos
  • Dashboard analytics and insights
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Any saved recommendations and data

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Account Information: Your Google account association, name, and email
  • All Images: Every photo you've uploaded or captured, stored on our Cloudinary servers
  • Detection History: All pest identification results and analytics data
  • App Data: Settings, preferences, and any cached information
  • Server Logs: Any logs containing your personal information

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7-14 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and all associated data have been permanently deleted from our systems.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your ScanPest AI account and all associated data, please send an email to our support team:
