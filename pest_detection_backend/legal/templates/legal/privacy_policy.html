Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Effective Date: June 26, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ScanPest AI collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered pest detection application.

Information We Collect

  • Images: We collect the images you capture or upload to identify pests. Images are stored securely on our servers using Cloudinary.
  • Detection History: We store your pest detection results and analytics data to provide dashboard insights and sync across devices.
  • Account Information: When you use Google Login, we collect basic profile information (name, email) to manage your account.

How We Use Information

  • To identify and classify pests using our AI technology
  • To provide personalized pesticide recommendations
  • To sync your detection history across all your devices
  • To generate analytics and insights in your dashboard
  • To provide customer support and app updates

Access to Files and Camera

Our app requires access to your device's camera and storage to enable core functionality:

  • Camera access for taking photos of pests
  • Storage access for local data storage and synchronization
  • Network access for syncing data and AI processing

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
  • Images are stored on secure Cloudinary servers with encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates

Deleting Your Data

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual detections from your dashboard
  • Contact us to request complete data deletion
  • Uninstalling the app removes local data

Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. Your information is only used for:

  • Pest detection and identification services
  • Providing your personal detection history
  • Customer support when you contact us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
